About us

Organization

HelthWyzer is a life sciences company committed to improving healthcare and life longevity. From prevention to treatment, HelthWyzer transforms scientific innovation into healthcare solutions, in human vaccines, rare diseases, immunology, and infectious diseases. Everyone at HelthWyzer is dedicated to make a difference in everyone's daily life, wherever they live and enable them to enjoy a healthier life. Our mission is to improve and extend people's lives.

Our heritage

In 2003, physician and drug store proprietor Dr. Oberheuser began producing accurate, scientifically formulated medications with the goal of providing more effective treatments to life-threatening diseases to patients and the physicians providing their care.

Under the pioneering leadership of Dr. Oberheuser, our company was among the founders of the compounds, expanding its business to meet rising health needs in the compounds by championing new areas of medical research. By continually entering new scientific areas, such as pigoon research, we have established a now long-standing tradition of helping people live happier, healthier, longer, and more beautiful lives.

Dr. Oberheuser's spirit of entrepreneurship, innovation, and caring lives on in our compound, culture, business, and contributions to medical science.
