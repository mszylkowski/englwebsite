HappiCuppa

Before HappiCuppa beans, normal coffee beans would ripen at different times in each separate bush; this meant they needed to be hand picked and processed, since close attention had to be payed to the bean. This inefficient system required a lot of manual labor and time, factors that both add costs to the company and to the consumer.

However, HappiCuppa solved this. The HappiCuppa bean is a genetically modified coffee bean designed so that all of the beans in a bush would ripen at the simultaneously. Thanks to this, coffee can be grown on huge plantations and harvested with machines. This makes shipping coffee beans more efficient and less costly, which in turn means it's less expensive for the customers.
