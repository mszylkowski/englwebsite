HappiCuppa

Before the HappiCuppa beans, coffee beans on each bush would ripen at different times and therefore needed to be hand picked and processed. This caused coffee beans to be shipped in small quantities making this system inefficient.

The HappiCuppa bean is a genetically modified coffee bean. It was designed so that all of the beans on a bush would ripen at the simultaneously, and coffee could be grown on huge plantations and harvested with machines. This makes shipping coffee beans more efficient, and less costly, which in turn, means less expensive for the customers.
